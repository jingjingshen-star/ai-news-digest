研究揭示"认知投降"现象：AI用户不加甄别地接受错误答案

要点：宾夕法尼亚大学研究发现，受试者获得AI辅助后，即便AI给出错误答案，仍有73.2%的概率不加审查地全盘接受；仅19.7%会主动纠正AI。金钱激励可提升纠正率19个百分点，但时间压力则使纠正率下降12个百分点。

Key Points: UPenn research found 73.2% of subjects uncritically accepted AI's faulty answers; only 19.7% corrected AI even when wrong. Financial incentives increased correction rates by 19 points, but time pressure decreased them by 12 points.

洞察：AI正在系统性地"外包"人类的批判性思维。高信任AI者恰恰是最容易被误导的群体。这意味着AI越普及，若无系统性媒介素养教育，人类整体判断力可能不升反降，民主社会的认知基础将被动摇。

Insight: AI is systematically 'outsourcing' human critical thinking. Those who trust AI most are most easily misled. As AI becomes more pervasive, without systematic media literacy education, overall human judgment may decline rather than improve, undermining the cognitive foundations of democratic society.
