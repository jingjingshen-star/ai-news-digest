Insight: OpenAI's 'robot tax' and public wealth fund proposals represent a proactive attempt to shape the social contract before superintelligence arrives — signaling that AI governance is entering the institutional design phase.

要点：Anthropic经济影响报告显示，AI尚未大规模替代工作，但早期影响已出现不均衡分布——年轻人受影响最大。CEO预测：AI可能在五年内消灭一半初级白领岗位，失业率或升至20%。AI正在奖励那些已经知道如何使用它的人。

洞察：AI尚未大规模替代工作，但已出现"AI技能鸿沟"——早期采用者正在积累不对称的竞争优势。如果这一趋势持续，AI不仅不会消除不平等，反而可能成为新的极化引擎。

Insight: An 'AI skills gap' is already emerging — early adopters are accumulating asymmetric competitive advantages. If this trend continues, AI may amplify rather than reduce social inequality.

卫星图像揭示：近40%的美国数据中心项目面临延期风险

Satellite & Drone Images Reveal 40% of US Data Centers May Face Construction Delays

要点：卫星和无人机图像分析显示，近40%的美国数据中心项目可能无法按计划今年完工。延期原因包括：劳动力短缺、电力不足、设备供应延迟及许可审批缓慢。社区对数据中心的土地使用、环境影响和电费上涨也有强烈抵制。

Key Points: Satellite and drone imagery analysis reveals that nearly 40% of US data center projects may fail to be completed on schedule this year. Causes include chronic labor shortages, power deficits, equipment delays, and slow permitting. Communities are also pushing back due to land use concerns, environmental impact, and rising electricity bills.

洞察：算力扩张遭遇物理瓶颈——40%的数据中心延期不仅影响AI公司产能计划，也将推高云计算价格。当能源和土地成为AI发展的硬约束，算力竞争逻辑将彻底改变。

Insight: 40% data center delays signal that AI expansion is hitting hard physical limits. This will raise cloud computing costs and fundamentally reshape the logic of AI compute competition — energy and land are becoming hard constraints.
